What is Mash-Up?

Mash-Up is a platform with a musician online community that allow every age and type of musician to join for free. All musician can create a band or find a companion for playing a music together. This platform also provide a space for practice room and the event organizer for the advertisement. Mash-Up is for everyone, and the most important, it for free. So let join us for a new dimension of a musician.

"Join us and become a part of the biggest musician online community platform."

Our Mission Statement

Our Mission is to gathering all musician together in one online community platform. For create a band and finding other musician to play with. In the future, we also offer a practice room booking and job application for the musician and a band that register in our platform.

Our Business Model

Our Business model aim to all musician in every age and all levels of expertise. In the future, we also aim for a practice room owner and event organizer for renting their practice room or hiring the musician or a band for playing in the event.
